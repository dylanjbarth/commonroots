Sun, 17 Jan 2021 13:54:46 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=5.9.2 Soil Advocate Training (by Kiss The Ground) https://www.commonroots.farm/2021/01/17/soil-advocate-training-by-kiss-the-ground/ https://www.commonroots.farm/2021/01/17/soil-advocate-training-by-kiss-the-ground/#respond Sun, 17 Jan 2021 13:52:04 +0000 http://www.commonrootz.com/?p=21 As a first step in our learning journey, we have signed up for the self-paced Soil Advocate Training course developed by Kiss the Ground (the non-profit with the same mission and name as the awesome documentary). They advertise the Soil Advocate Training as a way to learn more about the principles of regenerative agriculture and learn how to actually advocate for these principles with different audiences.

After purchasing access to the course ($149 at time of writing), we created a username and password allowing us to login to a portal that contained the course content. The course is organized into 8 modules and they recommend that you try and make your way through it at a sustainable pace, spending 1-2 hours on it per week and making it through the course in about 8 weeks. It looks like the first 6 modules are focused on the principles of regenerative agriculture and the last two address how you can spread the message effectively.

As we make progress through the course, we will be updating this main post with links to other posts summarizing what we are learning and linking out to other great learning resources.
